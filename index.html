Project Overview

Welcome to the future of plant care! Our Automated Water Pump system is designed for plant lovers who want healthy, happy plants without the hassle.

Let your plants thrive while you relax!

Key Features

  • Real-time moisture sensing Our sensors monitor soil moisture 24/7, ensuring your plants get water only when they need it.
  • Scheduled watering Set custom watering times and let our system handle the rest, even when you’re away.
  • Mobile notifications Get instant alerts on your phone if your plant needs attention or water is running low.
  • Energy efficient Our system uses minimal power, keeping your energy bills and environmental impact low.

How It Works

Our smart sensor checks your soil’s moisture level every hour. If your plant is thirsty, the pump gently waters it—no more, no less.

Set it and forget it. Your plants will thank you!
