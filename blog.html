5 Secrets to Healthier Houseplants

By Sarah Green • July 2025

Want thriving houseplants? Start with the basics: sunlight, water, and the right soil. But don’t forget about humidity and regular cleaning! Discover how our Auto Water Pump makes watering effortless and keeps your plants happy.

  • 1. Light: Place your plants where they get the right amount of sunlight. Too much or too little can harm them.
  • 2. Water: Water only when the soil is dry. Overwatering is a common mistake—our Auto Water Pump solves this by sensing soil moisture.
  • 3. Humidity: Many houseplants love humidity. Mist them or use a pebble tray for best results.
  • 4. Clean Leaves: Dust can block sunlight. Wipe leaves gently with a damp cloth every few weeks.
  • 5. Fertilize: Use a balanced fertilizer during the growing season for lush, healthy growth.

How Often Should You Water?

By Plantify Team • June 2025

Overwatering is the #1 killer of houseplants. Learn how our smart sensors take the guesswork out of watering, and get tips for different plant types and seasons.

Read More
  • Succulents & Cacti: Water every 2-3 weeks, letting soil dry completely between waterings.
  • Ferns & Tropical Plants: Keep soil consistently moist, but not soggy. Water when the top inch feels dry.
  • Seasonal Changes: Plants need less water in winter and more in summer. Adjust your schedule accordingly.
  • Smart Sensors: Our Auto Water Pump uses sensors to water only when needed, preventing root rot and stress.

Tip: Always check the soil before watering. With automation, you’ll never have to worry about over- or under-watering again!
